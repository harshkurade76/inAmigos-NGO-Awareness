Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization licensed by the Central Government, with its base in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

The foundation holds 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring tax-exempt benefits for donors, and is CSR-1 registered, allowing collaboration with corporate partners for impactful CSR initiatives. Additionally, we are NITI Aayog registered and hold the prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification.

With a strong network of dedicated professionals, volunteers, and corporate partners, we work tirelessly across India to bring positive change in society.
